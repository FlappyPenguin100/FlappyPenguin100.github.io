North Yorks Filey Library Website Book Recommendations This week I have a few book recommendations for people of different ages, I'll try always post this on a Saturday. 1-3 Early Readers books are a good idea for this sort of age, unless they excel with reading. They probably want to be reading either picture books with fewer words, or the early readers books. Of course, you can always read some of the easier junior books if you think you're up to it. Some of my favourites were:
  • Dear Zoo
  • The Tickle Monster
  • HUG
  • That's Not My Teddy!
4-6 This is sort of the age where you want to be reading between easier fully worded books, or some maybe more challenging. I have some recommendations here:
  • Spy Dog - Andrew Cope
  • Winnie the Witch - Valerie Thomas
  • The Magic Tree House - Mary Pope Osborne
  • Gargoylz - Jan Burchett & Sara Vogler
